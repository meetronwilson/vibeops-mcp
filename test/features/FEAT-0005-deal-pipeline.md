# Visual Deal Pipeline

**ID:** FEAT-0005 | **Module:** MOD-0003 | **Status:** in-progress | **Priority:** critical

Kanban-style visual pipeline for managing deals through sales stages with drag-and-drop functionality.

**Owner:** Amanda Rodriguez

## PRD Summary

### Problem
Sales teams struggle with managing multiple deals across different stages, lacking visual clarity on pipeline health and deal progression, which impacts forecasting accuracy and deal velocity.

### Goals
1. Provide visual representation of all deals in the pipeline
2. Enable easy deal stage progression with drag-and-drop
3. Give managers quick pipeline health visibility
4. Improve forecast accuracy through better stage management

### Success Metrics
- **Deal update frequency:** Deals updated at least weekly (90% compliance)
- **Forecast accuracy:** Within 15% of actual closed revenue
- **Pipeline visibility score:** Managers rate visibility 4.5/5 or higher

### Scope
**In Scope:**
- Kanban board view with customizable stages
- Drag-and-drop deal progression
- Deal cards with key information
- Filter and search deals
- Stage-based probability settings

**Out of Scope:**
- AI-powered deal scoring (future enhancement)
- Custom pipeline views per user (v2 feature)
- Automated stage progression rules

### Dependencies
- Deal entity data model must be finalized
- Company and contact modules must be operational
- Drag-and-drop UI library selection
